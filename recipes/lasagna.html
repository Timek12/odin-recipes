Lasagna recipe

Ingredients:
    9 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)
Preparation:
  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, and cook until the onions are softened.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Place three lasagna noodles on top.
  6. Add a layer of the cheese mixture, followed by a layer of meat sauce. Repeat these layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.

Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
